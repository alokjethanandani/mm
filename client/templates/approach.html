Design is an entrepreneurial activity

I validate ideas efficiently and effectively by putting them in front of customers early and often to make educated decisions.

The number one reason why new ideas fail is because entrepreneurs ignore their customers. To truly innovate, I get your product in front of your customers and validate market need. Rapid prototyping and customer development is how I get to answers and get there quickly.
